About Penga

Smart Financial Solutions For Every Goal

We have digitized Ikimina, the traditional community savings groups, and expanded them into a complete financial toolkit. Penga enables members to save together, lend within the group, and raise funds for life events. It supports structured group savings, collective investment management, and transparent capital allocation.
Penga tracks pooled capital across multiple ventures and provides dedicated accounting for each investment. Every project or business in the portfolio maintains clear financial records, accurate reporting, and transparent capital distribution. The platform combines two core functions: pooled resource management and venture-level investment tracking. It also includes integrated file management for secure organization of documents, reports, and records across both areas.

An Ikimina is a traditional rotating savings group where members contribute fixed amounts regularly and take turns receiving the pooled sum. Penga digitizes this by replacing cash with mobile money, tracking all contributions transparently on a dashboard, automating reminders, and enabling digital loan disbursements to members — all without requiring a traditional bank account.

Cloud funding is a digital group-financing tool that allows friends, families, and colleagues to pool money together for shared financial goals, community initiatives, or special events (like weddings or emergencies)

Absolutely. Penga operates under national financial regulations and employs bank-level encryption for all transactions. Group funds are held in regulated escrow accounts and cannot be accessed by unauthorized parties.

Groups can configure their loan policies — interest rates, repayment periods, and eligibility criteria. Once approved by group administrators, loans are disbursed digitally to the member's mobile wallet within minutes. All repayments are tracked in real time and visible to authorized group members.

No strict minimum. You can start a group with just 2 people for a personal savings partnership, or scale up to hundreds of members for large cooperatives or church communities. The platform adjusts to your group's size and needs.

Venture tracking provides dedicated bookkeeping and financial management for each investment within a portfolio. It ensures that every project, startup, or business maintains accurate financial records, transparent capital allocation, and reliable performance reporting.

Each venture's accounting records typically include:

  • Income Statement
  • Balance Sheet
  • Cash Flow Statement


This approach gives investors and stakeholders clear visibility into the financial health, profitability, and cash position of every investment.

The Group Management Committee includes a centralized document management system for securely storing and organizing important records such as group bylaws, meeting minutes, policies, agreements, and other official documents.

The Secretary is responsible for managing document access and visibility. Access permissions can be assigned to specific users or user groups, ensuring that sensitive information is available only to authorized individuals. This provides secure document sharing, controlled access, and efficient record management across the organization.
